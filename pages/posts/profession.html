PROFESSION

Google

Google organizes the world's information and makes it universally accessible and useful. Some of the IT roles they offer are: Software Engineer, Product Manager, Sales Engineer, Technical Solutions, etc. Google uses different technologies like: HTML5 , Google Analytics , jQuery , GStatic Google Static Content and QUIC. The company also emphasizes the importance of openness among employees, as a way of promoting innovation.

In conclusion, Google has many job offers and they provide various benefits and flexibility to their workers. Besides, they have companies in various locations, which provides growth opportunities. I like that Google organizes internal activities to maintain a healthy and balanced life for their employees. Also, that they get to work in nice places and with a well paid salary.

Nintendo

Nintendo develops, produces and markets software and hardware. Some of the IT roles they offer are: Data & Insights Analyst, Software Engineer, Gameplay Engineer, Associate Engineer, etc. Nintendo uses different technologies like: C, C#, C++, SQL and Python. Nintendo's company culture is a celebration of both work and play, with events and activities galore.

In conclusion, Nintendo is a company that cares about the well-being of its employees and is looking for many software and game engineers. I like that they create activities and events for the employees. Also, that I could learn and create video games or hardware.

Alibaba

Alibaba is the world's biggest online commerce company. Some of the IT roles they offer are: Cloud Intelligence Business Development Manager, Cloud Senior Solutions Architect, Software Engineer, etc. Alibaba uses Taobao, which is based in different technologies like: PHP, CSS, HTML and JS. Alibaba has an established organizational culture based on the value of all employees.

In conclusion, Alibaba is one of the best paid companies. It owns all the e-commerce in China. They also have several job opportunities and the orgranization is based on a community with values. I like that it is in China since I lived there for three years. Also, If I work there I would learn more about IT and also about business.
